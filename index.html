Edward Gadson

Owner/Head Instructor at Warrior's Path Karate

I have been training in Okinawan Shorin Ryu for the past twenty years,
and I have decided that sharing my knowledge by teaching others is the best use
of my life. The things I've learned about myself, others, and being a human
being has been of inestimable value to me, and I want to share that
knowledge and awareness with others.

Dojo History

I started my martial arts journey at Gabriele's Karate in Groton, CT in May of 2001, where after two years of training, I was invited to become a student teacher. I stayed on teaching and training until shortly after my second degree black belt test in December of 2007. From there, I found my current teacher, Renshi Cliff Cochran of Shorin Ryu Karate of Connecticut, formerly East Coast Karate, in Norwich, CT. He taught and mentored me until I was able to open Warrior's Path Karate in December of 2016. He remains my teacher and mentor to this day.

So, to list them:

  • Gabriele's Karate: 2001-2007
  • Shorin Ryu Karate of Connecticut: 2007-2016
  • Warrior's Path Karate: 2016-present
